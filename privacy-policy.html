Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Assam Jobs, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Assam Jobs and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account to browse job listings. However, we use your browser's Local Storage to allow you to save articles to a "Favorites" list. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase to host our database of job updates and articles. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data to ensure the platform runs securely and efficiently.

3. Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the internet.
  • Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. External Links

Our website contains links to other websites (such as official government portals or application forms). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites or services.

5. Contact Us
